--- Dry brush stone.
05) Ulthuan Grey. Dry brush edges.
06) Tidy up the base with Abaddon Black
07) Steel Legion Drab. Overbrush base...
08) Shade Seraphim Sepia. On and around rocks.
09) Zandri Dust. Dry brush rocks and sand on base.
10) Army Painter Desert Yellow / Citadel Karak Stone.
--- Dry brush edges of base/stones/sand
11) Screaming Skull. Dry brush rocks on base. Again.
12) Agrax Earth Shade. Shade a bit where Stone/Sand and wall meet.
13) Averland Sunset.
--- If desired, do another dry brush on base for a slightly more cartoonish look.
